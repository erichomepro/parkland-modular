🔧 Module 1

Production & Quality Assurance

Every trailer has a master checklist. Every task gets assigned. Every completion gets documented with photos and signatures. When a trailer is "done," it's really done — fully documented, fully verified, zero question marks.

How a Trailer Moves Through the System

1

PO Created → Trailer Registered

New purchase order comes in. Trailers are added to the system with their type (sleeper, kitchen, washcar, office). The master checklist auto-generates based on trailer type — every task that needs to happen, pre-loaded.

2

Tasks Assigned to Workers

Manager assigns tasks to team members. Each worker opens their phone and sees exactly what's on their plate today. Dependencies are enforced — electrical can't start until framing is signed off.

3

Work Done → Photo + Signature

Worker completes a task, takes a photo of the finished work, and signs off with their finger on screen. Timestamp, location, and worker identity are all captured automatically.

4

Manager Reviews & Approves

Manager sees task completions in real time. Reviews the photo evidence, approves or sends back for rework with a note. No task moves to "complete" without manager sign-off.

5

Trailer Complete — Full Documentation Package

When every task is ✓ and every sign-off is in, the trailer is officially complete. The system generates a full documentation package: every task, every photo, every signature, every timestamp. If a client calls in 6 months, you pull up the record.

🚛 Smart Feature

The Morning Run Sheet — One Trip, Not Ten

Every night, the AI scans tomorrow's tasks, checks what's in stock, and generates a consolidated pickup list. One person, one trip, everything the whole team needs. No more forgotten parts. No more wasted hours at the store.

How It Works

  • 🌙
    AI Scans Overnight At 4 AM, the system checks tomorrow's tasks across all trailers, cross-references with current stock levels, and identifies everything that's needed but not available.
  • 🗺
    Grouped By Store, Optimized By Route Items are grouped by supplier. The system knows Fastenal opens at 7, Home Depot at 8. Hit them in the right order.
  • 📲
    Sent to the Runner's Phone The designated runner gets a push notification. Opens the app, sees the list, gets the job done in one efficient trip.
  • 📷
    Receipt Capture on Return Runner photographs receipts, checks off items as received. Materials auto-sign into inventory and allocate to the right POs. Admin sees it immediately.
⏰
Save 2-3 Hours of Downtime Per Day No more workers stopping mid-task to drive to the store. No more 3 people making separate trips to the same place. One run, before work starts, everything ready.

🛡 Module 4

Safety & Compliance — The Biggest Deal

This is where the platform earns its place. Better safety = bigger contracts, lower insurance, zero incidents. The AI knows every regulation, every SOP, every PPE requirement. Workers are protected. The company is protected. Everyone goes home safe.

🛡

Nobody Gets Hurt. Nobody Gets Forgotten.

Every worker, every shift, every task — the system makes sure the right safety procedures are followed before any work begins. Not because we don't trust people, but because people deserve a system that has their back.

The Daily Safety Flow

1
☀️

Start of Shift

Worker opens app, confirms they are fit for duty — not fatigued, not impaired, ready to work. Digital signature locks it in.

2
📋

Task Safety Brief

AI shows: "For welding on Trailer 312, you need: welding helmet, leather gloves, fire-resistant jacket, safety glasses." Worker checks off each item.

3
🤖

Ask the AI

"Is it safe to grind near the propane hookup?" AI checks specs, provides guidance, flags for supervisor if needed. Instant expert advice.

4
✅

Acknowledge & Work

Worker confirms they have the right PPE and understand the hazards. Sign off. Start work. Every acknowledgment is stored with timestamp.

Pre-Trip Inspections

Every vehicle and piece of equipment gets a digital walk-around before it's used. Checklists auto-generated per vehicle type. Flag an issue and it becomes a maintenance ticket instantly.

Incident Reporting

Near-miss or incident? Guided form walks through what happened, where, who was involved, photos of the scene. Auto-escalates based on severity. Tracks corrective actions until they're closed.

Certification Tracking

The system knows every worker's certifications and when they expire. "Mike's confined space cert expires in 14 days" — the alert goes to the manager AND admin before it lapses.
